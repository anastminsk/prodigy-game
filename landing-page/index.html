Prodigy Fighting Game

Description

Prodigy Fighting Game is a simple clone of the famous game in which you must fight against virtual enemy answering different logic questions. You can customise the appearance of your enemy, the attack type and choose questions to answer.

There are 2 attack types and 5 types of tasks: mathematics, translation, listening, cities and animals. Your should solve as many tasks as possible killing enemies. You and enemy have 4 lives at each level. If you have no more lives, you lose. If your enemy has no more lives, you win and can struggle with the next enemy.

Let's start to play and show your brain abilities!
